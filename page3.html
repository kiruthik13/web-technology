IR20 rice offers you a unique aroma and incredible taste. Harvested from rich Indian paddy fields, 100% natural and has won several hearts across our region. It is perfect for making SPONGY, SOFT and DELICIOUS idly, crispy dosa, etc., which gives divine feeling to your taste buds. Ideal rice to start your day with. IR-20 stands for International Rice-20.It's a high yielding variety of rice developed by the International Rice Research Institute. This variety of rice is white colored, semi-dwarf, medium slender.

IR64 is a variety of raw rice that is widely grown and consumed in India. It is a medium-grain rice that is known for its high yield and good cooking quality, and is often used for both household consumption and commercial purposes.

Basmati is a fragrant Indian rice with an intoxicating natural aroma. Its nutty, buttery flavor pairs well with savory dishes like curries and other Middle Eastern and Asian fare. White Basmati Rice is lighter and fluffier than traditional white rice and has a more concentrated flavor than the jasmine variety.
